Account Structure

How it all connects

8x8 acts as your BSP (Business Solution Provider) — we have On-Behalf-Of (OBO) permissions to send traffic, but you retain full ownership of your assets.

One FBM. Multiple WABAs. Unlimited reach.

Your Meta Business Portfolio (FBM) is the top-level container for all your WhatsApp assets. Under it, each WABA (WhatsApp Business Account) can hold multiple phone numbers and templates.

  • 1 FBM can hold up to 20 WABAs by default
  • Each WABA has its own quality rating and messaging tier
  • Phone numbers under a WABA: unlimited (capped by FBM verification status)
  • Unverified FBM: max 2 numbers. Verified FBM: up to 20 (extendable)
  • 8x8 connects via Embedded Sign-Up — you keep ownership of your WABA
