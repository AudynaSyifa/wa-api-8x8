Step by Step

Setup Steps

Follow the steps for your chosen path. Do not skip steps — each one depends on the previous.

1

Create Facebook Business Page & Meta Business Portfolio

Set up your Meta Business Portfolio — the top-level container for all your WhatsApp assets. Must be 100% verified before proceeding to Step 2.

~1 day⚠ Required before Step 2
2

Complete Business Verification

Submit legal documents (e.g. Akta Pendirian, NPWP, SIUP) via Meta Business Manager → Security Center. Meta reviews within 1–3 business days. Unlocks messaging tier from 250 → 2,000 unique users/day.

1–3 business days
3

Embedded Sign-Up via 8x8 Connect

Log in to 8x8 Connect portal → Messaging Apps → Create WA Account. Complete Embedded Sign-Up: connect your Meta account, enter display name, business profile, and phone number. Verify via OTP. Display name review takes 1–2 business days.

~1–2 days
▶ Interactive Sign-Up Walkthrough
4

Create & Submit Message Templates + API Integration

Build your Auth, Utility, and Marketing templates in 8x8 Connect. Meta's AI reviews and approves within minutes to hours. Integrate the 8x8 API to your system and run end-to-end UAT testing.

Minutes–hours (template approval)
📄 Template Management Guide
5

Go Live 🎉

Enable 2FA, validate test sends, and you're live! Monitor quality rating and delivery in 8x8 Connect and Meta Business Manager. Optionally apply for OBA (Blue Tick) 30 days after go-live.

Go live!
1
Request 2FA Disable from Current BSP
Critical — must do first

Contact your current BSP (Twilio, Vonage, Infobip, etc.) and request them to disable 2FA on your WABA. They do this from their Meta Business Manager. Migration cannot proceed without this.

What to tell your BSP: "We are migrating our WABA to a new BSP. Please disable the 2FA/two-step verification on our WABA ID [your WABA ID] so we can proceed with the migration."
2
Log in to 8x8 Connect Portal
Action required

Go to connect.8x8.com → Messaging Apps → Create WhatsApp Account and select the Migration option (not Embedded Sign-Up).

3
Complete the Migration Form
Action required

Fill in all required fields in the 8x8 Connect migration form.

Information to enter:

– Phone number you are migrating
– Display name (must match what is approved on Meta)
– Business profile details (description, category, website, etc.)
– Select subaccount where the channel will be placed
4
Log in with Facebook & Select Your Business
Action required

During the flow, you'll be redirected to a Facebook login step. Log in with the admin account for the Meta Business Manager where your WABA currently sits, then select the correct Business Portfolio.

⚠️ Use the same Facebook account that has admin access to the Meta BM where your current WABA lives.
5
Verify via OTP
Action required

An OTP will be sent to the phone number being migrated via SMS or voice call. Enter the OTP code in the portal to verify ownership.

💡 If OTP is not received, confirm the number is active. Try switching between SMS and voice call options.
6
Select Number & Subaccount, Complete Setup
Action required

Confirm the phone number and select the subaccount where the migrated WABA channel will be placed on the 8x8 side. Complete the setup flow.

7
Re-enable 2FA on 8x8 Side
Post-migration

Once migration is complete and live on 8x8, re-enable 2FA from Meta Business Manager → WhatsApp Manager → Settings. This secures your WABA on the new BSP.

🔒 Important: Always re-enable 2FA after migration is confirmed live. Leaving it disabled is a security risk.
8
Validate & Go Live 🎉
Migration complete

Send test messages to confirm the channel is working. Verify your templates, API credentials, and webhook endpoints are pointing to 8x8. Your messaging tier, quality rating, and approved templates carry over automatically.

Troubleshooting

Common Blockers & How to Resolve

Select your path to see the most relevant blockers.

1

FBM Not Verified

Meta Business Portfolio is incomplete or pending — you cannot proceed until 100% verified.

Go to Meta Business Manager → Security Center → Start Verification. Use company domain email. Prepare: Akta Pendirian, NPWP, or SIUP. Review takes 1–3 business days.
2

Phone Number Already Registered on WhatsApp

The number is already linked to an existing WhatsApp account, blocking OTP verification.

Option A: Delete the existing WA account from the app first, then wait 24 hours. Option B: Use a fresh, never-registered number.
3

Display Name "Under Review" for More Than 2 Days

Meta's display name review normally takes 1–2 business days. If it's longer, escalation is needed.

Raise a Meta Direct Support ticket with: WABA ID, phone number, sign-up date, and business category. Go to Meta Business Manager → Help → Contact Support.
4

OTP Not Received

OTP not received during Embedded Sign-Up, preventing phone number activation.

Verify the number is active. Confirm with your telco (e.g. Telkomsel, Indosat). Try switching between SMS and voice call OTP options.
5

Template Rejected or Recategorized to Marketing

Template was rejected, or Meta's AI reclassified it from Utility to Marketing — affecting pricing and delivery.

Review template for promotional language. Utility templates must be strictly transactional. You have 60 days to appeal a recategorization via Meta Business Manager.
6

Quality Rating Dropped to Yellow or Red

Messaging quality has declined, risking tier downgrade and reduced daily reach.

Go to Meta Business Manager → WhatsApp Manager → Quality. Clean your opt-in list, pause low-performing templates, and review message timing. Yellow = act within 7 days to avoid tier downgrade.
1
2FA Not Disabled by Current BSP
▾
The most common migration failure. If 2FA is still enabled on the current BSP side, OTP verification will fail and migration cannot complete.

Resolution: Re-coordinate with your current BSP to disable 2FA. Confirm they have done this before retrying. Once disabled, proceed immediately — some BSPs may re-enable it automatically.
2
OTP Not Received During Migration
▾
OTP is sent to the phone number being migrated. If not received, it could be a telco issue or the number is temporarily inactive.

Resolution: Confirm the number is active and reachable. Try switching between SMS OTP and Voice Call OTP. Check with the telco operator if needed.
3
Meta Business Portfolio Not Verified
▾
If the Meta Business Portfolio is not 100% verified, the migration flow will be blocked when connecting the Meta account.

Resolution: Go to Meta Business Manager → Security Center → Start Verification. Prepare legal documents (Akta Pendirian, NPWP, or SIUP). Verification typically takes 1–3 business days.
4
Display Name Mismatch After Migration
▾
The display name in 8x8 Connect differs from what appears in Meta Business Manager.

Resolution: Contact 8x8 support to manually sync the display name. Provide your WABA ID, Channel ID, the correct display name as shown in Meta BM, and phone number.
5
Templates Missing After Migration
▾
Approved templates may take a few minutes to appear in 8x8 Connect after migration since they are stored at the WABA level in Meta.

Resolution: Wait 15–30 minutes after migration completes and refresh the Templates section. If still missing after 1 hour, contact 8x8 support with your WABA ID.
