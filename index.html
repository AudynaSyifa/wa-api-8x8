Troubleshooting

Common Blockers & How to Resolve

The most frequent issues customers encounter during onboarding. Check here before escalating to support.

1

FBM Not Verified

Meta Business Portfolio is incomplete or pending. This is the most common onboarding blocker — you cannot proceed until 100% verified.

Go to Meta Business Manager → Security Center → Start Verification. Use company domain email. Prepare: Akta Pendirian, NPWP, or SIUP. Review takes 1–3 business days.
2

Phone Number Already Registered on WhatsApp

The number is already linked to an existing WhatsApp account, blocking OTP verification during sign-up.

Option A: Delete the existing WhatsApp account from the WA app first, then wait 24 hours. Option B: Use a fresh, never-registered number for the smoothest experience.
3

Display Name "Under Review" for More Than 2 Days

Meta's display name review normally takes 1–2 business days. If it's been longer with no update, escalation is needed.

Raise a Meta Direct Support ticket with: WABA ID, phone number, sign-up date, and business category. Go to Meta Business Manager → Help → Contact Support.
4

OTP Not Received

OTP verification SMS or voice call not received during Embedded Sign-Up, preventing phone number activation.

Verify the number is active and can receive SMS or voice calls. Confirm with your telco operator (e.g. Telkomsel, Indosat). Try switching between SMS and voice call OTP options.
5

Template Rejected or Recategorized to Marketing

Template was rejected, or Meta's AI reclassified it from Utility to Marketing — affecting pricing and delivery.

Review template for promotional language. Utility templates must be strictly transactional. Remove any promotional calls-to-action. You have 60 days to appeal a recategorization via Meta Business Manager.
6

Quality Rating Dropped to Yellow or Red

Messaging quality has declined, risking tier downgrade and reduced daily reach.

Go to Meta Business Manager → WhatsApp Manager → Quality. Review block reasons. Clean your opt-in list, review message timing and relevance, and pause low-performing templates. Yellow = act within 7 days to avoid tier downgrade.

Error Reference

Common Error Codes

Quick reference for the most frequently seen API errors. For the full list, visit Meta's official error code documentation.

Error Code Meaning Resolution
131026 Phone number not registered on WhatsApp Verify recipient is an active WhatsApp user. Check via wa.me/[number]
470 Template not approved or paused Check template status in 8x8 Connect. Re-submit if rejected. Never retry with a paused template.
131047 24-hour service window expired Use a pre-approved template to re-initiate the conversation. Freeform not allowed outside the window.
130429 Rate limit hit — messaging tier exceeded Reduce send speed. Check messaging tier and quality rating in Meta Business Manager. Do not retry blindly.
131000 Generic / unknown error Retry the request. If persists, check Meta Status Page and raise a support ticket.
Stuck at "sent" (1 tick) Recipient has blocked your number No fix available — Meta hides block status for privacy. Message will never deliver.
