Overview

Migration vs. New Onboarding

Migration lets you keep your existing phone number and WABA history. Here's how it differs from a fresh onboarding.

ℹ️
Migration is the process of moving an existing WhatsApp Business Account from another Business Solution Provider (BSP) — such as Twilio, Vonage, Infobip, or Froged — to 8x8, while retaining the same phone number and WABA ID.
🆕
New Onboarding Fresh start
  • New phone number required
  • Fresh WABA ID generated
  • No messaging history carried over
  • Messaging tier starts at Tier 1 (2,000/day)
  • No 2FA disable step needed
  • Cannot keep existing number
🔄
WABA Migration Recommended
  • Keep your existing phone number
  • Retain existing WABA ID
  • Quality rating & tier carried over
  • Approved templates remain valid
  • Minimal customer disruption
  • Requires 2FA disable from current BSP

Step by Step

Migration Steps

Follow these steps in order. Do not skip the 2FA step — attempting to migrate without it will fail.

1
Request 2FA Disable from Current BSP
Critical — must do first

Contact your current BSP (Twilio, Vonage, Infobip, Froged, etc.) and request them to disable 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication) on your WABA. They will do this from their Meta Business Manager. This step is mandatory — migration cannot proceed without it.

What to tell your BSP: "We are migrating our WABA to a new BSP. Please disable the 2FA/two-step verification on our WABA ID [your WABA ID] so we can proceed with the migration."
2
Log in to 8x8 Connect Portal
Action required

Go to connect.8x8.com and log in with your 8x8 account. Navigate to Messaging Apps → Create WhatsApp Account and select the Migration option (not Embedded Sign-Up).

3
Complete the Migration Flow on 8x8 Connect
Action required

Fill in all required information in the 8x8 Connect portal migration form.

Information to enter:

– Phone number you are migrating
– Display name (must match what is approved on Meta)
– Business profile details (description, category, website, etc.)
– Select subaccount where the channel will be placed
4
Log in with Facebook & Select Your Business
Action required

During the flow, you will be redirected to a Facebook login step. Log in with the Facebook account that has admin access to your Meta Business Manager, then select the correct verified Business Portfolio.

⚠️ Use the same Facebook account / email that has admin access to the Meta BM where your WABA currently sits.
5
Verify via OTP
Action required

An OTP will be sent to the phone number being migrated — either via SMS or voice call. Enter the OTP code in the portal to verify ownership of the number.

💡 If OTP is not received, confirm the number is active and can receive calls/SMS. Try switching between SMS and voice call options.
6
Select Number & Subaccount, Complete Setup
Action required

Confirm the phone number and select the subaccount where the migrated WABA channel will be placed on the 8x8 side. Complete the setup flow.

7
Re-enable 2FA on 8x8 Side
Post-migration

Once migration is complete and the channel is live on 8x8, re-enable 2FA (Two-Step Verification) from your Meta Business Manager → WhatsApp Manager → Settings. This secures your WABA on the new BSP.

🔒 Important: Always re-enable 2FA after migration is confirmed live. Leaving it disabled is a security risk.
8
Validate & Go Live 🎉
Migration complete

Send test messages to confirm the channel is working correctly on 8x8. Verify your templates, API credentials, and webhook endpoints are pointing to 8x8. Your messaging tier, quality rating, and approved templates will carry over automatically.

Troubleshooting

Common Migration Blockers

The most frequent issues encountered during WABA migration and how to resolve them.

1
2FA Not Disabled by Current BSP
▾
The most common migration failure. If 2FA is still enabled on the current BSP side, the OTP verification step will fail and migration cannot complete.

Resolution: Re-coordinate with your current BSP to disable 2FA. Confirm they have done this before retrying the migration flow. Once 2FA is disabled, proceed immediately — don't wait too long as some BSPs may re-enable it automatically.
2
OTP Not Received During Migration
▾
OTP is sent to the phone number being migrated. If not received, it could be a telco issue or the number is temporarily inactive.

Resolution: Confirm the number is active and reachable. Try switching between SMS OTP and Voice Call OTP options in the portal. Check with the telco operator if needed.
3
Meta Business Portfolio Not Verified
▾
If the Meta Business Portfolio is not 100% verified, the migration flow will be blocked when connecting the Meta account.

Resolution: Go to Meta Business Manager → Security Center → Start Verification. Prepare legal documents (Akta Pendirian, NPWP, or SIUP for Indonesian businesses). Verification typically takes 1–3 business days.
4
Display Name Mismatch After Migration
▾
Sometimes the display name shown in the 8x8 Connect portal differs from what appears in Meta Business Manager. This can happen if different names were entered at different steps.

Resolution: Contact 8x8 support to manually sync the display name. Provide your WABA ID, Channel ID, the correct display name as shown in Meta BM, and phone number. Wait for Meta approval before requesting the update.
5
Templates Missing After Migration
▾
Approved templates from the previous BSP should carry over since they are stored at the WABA level in Meta, not the BSP level. However, they may take a few minutes to appear in 8x8 Connect after migration.

Resolution: Wait 15–30 minutes after migration completes and refresh the Templates section in 8x8 Connect. If templates are still missing after 1 hour, contact 8x8 support with your WABA ID.
